# Real-World Use Cases

## 1. Urban Commuter

A woman traveling late at night uses SOMA to share her live location and send an SOS if she feels unsafe. Her partner receives instant alerts and can track her status in real time.

## 2. Rural/Low Network

A user in a rural area triggers SOS during a network outage. The app caches the alert and sends it automatically when connectivity returns, ensuring no call for help is lost.

## 3. Health & Well-being

A user logs daily symptoms and cycle data, gaining insights into her health trends and receiving personalized tips.

## 4. Community Support

A user joins a group chat for local safety tips and support, building trust and resilience in her community.

## 5. Emergency Escalation

If a partner does not respond to an SOS, the app escalates the alert to backup contacts or local authorities.

---

> _SOMA adapts to real-world needs, making safety and support accessible everywhere._
