# PM Case Study: SOMA – Women Safety App

## 1. Why This Product Was Built

Women’s safety is a persistent, global issue. Existing solutions are fragmented, lack real-time support, and often compromise privacy. SOMA was conceived to provide a holistic, trustworthy, and scalable safety platform.

## 2. Market Gap

- Most apps focus only on SOS, ignoring well-being and community
- Lack of privacy-first, real-time solutions
- Poor support for low-network or offline scenarios

## 3. Target Users

- Women (urban & rural)
- Trusted partners/family
- NGOs, safety orgs

## 4. Pain Points

- Inability to get instant help
- Lack of trusted network integration
- Data privacy concerns
- No holistic well-being support

## 5. Solution Thinking

- Unified platform: SOS + health + community
- Real-time partner notifications
- Privacy by design
- Offline/low-network support

## 6. Trade-offs

- Real-time features vs. battery/data usage
- Simplicity vs. feature richness
- Privacy vs. analytics

## 7. Scalability

- Modular, cloud-native architecture
- Supabase for auto-scaling
- Stateless client for easy updates

## 8. Impact

- Faster SOS response times
- Improved user trust & retention
- Real-world safety outcomes

## 9. Why This Matters

Empowering women with reliable, private, and actionable safety tools can save lives and foster societal change.
