# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Purpose

SOMA aims to provide women with a reliable, real-time safety and well-being platform, bridging the gap between emergency response, health tracking, and trusted community support.

## 2. Background

Despite advances in technology, women’s safety remains a global concern. Existing apps lack real-time support, privacy, or holistic well-being features. SOMA addresses these gaps with a unified, privacy-first solution.

## 3. Objectives & Goals

- Enable rapid emergency response (SOS)
- Foster trusted networks (partner linking)
- Support daily well-being (cycle, symptom tracking)
- Deliver actionable insights
- Ensure privacy and data security

## 4. Target Users

- Women (primary)
- Trusted partners/family
- NGOs, safety organizations

## 5. Features (MVP)

- SOS alert & location sharing
- Partner/trusted contact linking
- Daily check-in & symptom log
- Cycle tracking
- Insights dashboard
- Secure authentication

## 6. Success Metrics

- User retention & engagement
- SOS response time
- Number of linked partners
- Daily log completion rate
- User-reported safety improvement

## 7. Constraints

- Low network reliability
- Data privacy regulations
- Device compatibility

## 8. Future Enhancements

- Real-time chat/help
- AI safety assistant
- Community safety network

## 9. Why This Matters

Women’s safety is a fundamental right. SOMA empowers users with tools, support, and peace of mind, driving real-world impact.
