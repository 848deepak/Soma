# Why This Matters

## Real-World Impact

- Women face daily safety risks—SOMA provides tools that can save lives.
- Empowerment through technology: users gain control, confidence, and support.

## Use Cases

- Emergency: Immediate SOS, location sharing, and partner notification.
- Daily life: Health tracking, insights, and community support.
- Low network: Offline-first design ensures help is always available.

## Edge Cases

- App works in low/no network by caching critical actions (SOS, logs).
- Emergency escalation if no response from partner.

## Trust & Privacy

- User data is private, encrypted, and user-controlled.
- Open source for transparency and trust.

## Leadership & Scalability

- Modular, cloud-native architecture for global reach.
- Designed for real-world adoption and societal change.

---

> _SOMA is more than an app—it’s a movement for safety, trust, and empowerment._
