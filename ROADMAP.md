# Roadmap: SOMA – Women Safety App

## Phase 1: MVP

- SOS alert & location sharing
- Partner/trusted contact linking
- Daily check-in & symptom log
- Cycle tracking
- Insights dashboard
- Secure authentication
- Privacy-first data handling

## Phase 2: Scale

- Real-time chat/help feature
- Group/community support
- Advanced analytics & insights
- Enhanced offline support
- Localization (multi-language)

## Phase 3: AI Integration

- AI safety assistant (chatbot)
- Threat detection & smart alerts
- Predictive safety recommendations
- Integration with local authorities/NGOs

## Ongoing

- Security & privacy enhancements
- Accessibility improvements
- Community engagement

> _From MVP to global impact: building a safer future, together._
