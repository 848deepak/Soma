# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism
- Show empathy toward others

## Our Responsibilities

- Enforce standards of acceptable behavior
- Remove unacceptable behavior

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and in public spaces when representing the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the maintainers.

---

> _Together, we build a safer, more inclusive world._
